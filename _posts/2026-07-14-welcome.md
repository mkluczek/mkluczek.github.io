Welcome to my corner of the web. I'm Marcin — I lead the Earth Observation
Algorithms team at CloudFerro, where we're turning petabytes of satellite
data into something you can actually *use*: embeddings, foundation models,
semantic search and anomaly detection over the whole planet.

This blog is where I'll share notes on **GeoAI** — foundation models for
Earth Observation, embeddings at scale (like [Major TOM](https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2412.05600)),
and the messy, practical engineering that makes AI-for-EO work in production.

More soon. For now, the site is still **under construction** — expect it to
grow.

## What to expect here

- **Foundation models & embeddings** — what works, what doesn't, at scale
- **Practical GeoAI** — from research prototype to production pipeline
- **Field notes** — papers, talks and things I learn along the way

Thanks for stopping by.
